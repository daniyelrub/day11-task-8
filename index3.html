Arnold Schwarzenegger - Governor of California

Tenure as Governor

Arnold Schwarzenegger served as the Governor of California from November 17, 2003, to January 3, 2011. During his tenure, he focused on various issues such as climate change, healthcare reform, and the state budget.

Arnold Schwarzenegger as Governor of California

He was known for his bipartisan approach and worked with both Democrats and Republicans to address key issues facing the state. Schwarzenegger's time in office was marked by several notable achievements, including the implementation of AB 32, a landmark climate change law.
