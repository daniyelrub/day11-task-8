Directed by Benjamin Ree.[537][538] Carlsen is an avid fan of football, with Real Madrid CF as his favourite club. In recognition of becoming world chess champion, he took the honorary kick-off in a La Liga game between Real Madrid and Real Valladolid on 30 November 2013.[531] Carlsen also follows the Premier League and plays fantasy football. In December 2019, he reached the No. 1 spot on a Fantasy Premier League game, ahead of seven million other players, before eventually finishing the season in 10th place.[532][533] In April 2022, Carlsen played poker at the Norwegian Championships Main Event and finished 25th out of 1050 players.[534][535]
