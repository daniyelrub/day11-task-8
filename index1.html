Early Life and Bodybuilding Beginnings

Arnold Schwarzenegger's passion for bodybuilding began at a young age. He started weight training at the age of 15 and quickly made a name for himself in the sport. In 1968, at the age of 20, he won the Mr. Universe title, becoming the youngest person ever to achieve the feat. This victory marked the beginning of his illustrious bodybuilding career, which would see him dominate the sport for years to come.

Throughout the 1970s, Arnold continued to compete and win numerous bodybuilding competitions, including five Mr. Olympia titles. His dedication to training and his iconic physique made him a legendary figure in the bodybuilding world.
